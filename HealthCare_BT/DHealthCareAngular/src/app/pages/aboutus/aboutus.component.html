about us

Since our establishment in 2010, Alpha Health GP has been offering high quality healthcare solutions to a wide range of customers, both individual and corporate.

Alpha Health is a synonym of professionalism and top-notch medical services. We strive for excellence in everything we do, that’s why our clients prefer our clinic to other medical companies, and our staff is the key to our success.

Whether you need a medical consultation or solution to any medical issue, our company and its specialists are always ready to help. We care about your health and treatment of any diseases you or your relatives may have.

Our medical company has been numerously awarded for our top quality services and dedication to what we do. The reviews of our clients prove our professionalism.
